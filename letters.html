Hey, You!

Yes, you, I know how it all feels,
the gender euphoria,
the depression,
all the uncertainty in between, amplified by my youth.
yep, all of it. Or at the very least a pretty good deal of it. Unfortunately, none of it sounds very stable. Identifying as a transgender individual during your youth is difficult. The turmoil it can cause, the bullying , the thoughts racing through your head. Friend, if you take anything from this website, it's that you should keep yourself safe. Have your small community, find little things that bring you joy.
Take up a new name,
put on some makeup,
throw on some new clothes.
Pick yourself up and find joy in the little things that make you You.
