Artwork: Dysphoria

A gender dysphoria diagnosis never felt liek it fixed anything for me. I never felt man enough when I had bouts of dysphoria. It caused me anxiety, and I felt like I could not trust the reflection in my mirror.

Artwork: Euhporia

For me, gender euphoria was the warm feeling I got whenever my preferred gender was validated. This image represents that with the soft lighting hitting the subject.

Artwork: DEtransition

The right decision for me was letting go of my old identity to embrace my femininity. Moths are used to showcase the death of my past self.
